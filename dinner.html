3. Sausage & Peppers Skillet

ingredients: 1 package pre-cooked Italian sausage,sliced veggies 2 bell peppers, 1 large onion, 1 tablespoon olive oil, salt, and pepper

Steps:

  1. Prep: Slice the sausage, peppers, and onion.
  2. Saut√©:Heat the olive oil in a large skillet (or sheet pan) over medium-high heat. Add the peppers and onions, and cook for about 5-7 minutes until they start to soften.
  3. Sausage: Add the sliced sausage to the pan with the vegetables.
  4. Cooking: Cook for another 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sausage is nicely browned and heated through, and the vegetables are tender-crisp. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve:Serve the mix as-is, or pile it into hot dog buns, over rice, or with a scoop of pasta sauce on the side!
