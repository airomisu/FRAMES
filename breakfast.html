Breakfast

1. French Toast

ingredients: bread, 2 eggs, 1/4 cup of milk, a dash of cinnamon or vanilla, butter or oil

Steps:

  1. Mix: Whisk eggs, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla in a shallow dish.
  2. Heat:Melt a little butter/oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Dip: Quickly dip each side of a bread slice into the egg mix.
  4. Cook: Place in the hot pan. Cook 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown.
  5. Serve:Repeat with the rest of the slices. Top with syrup and enjoy!

2. Pancake

ingredients: 1 cup all-purpose flour, 1 cup milk, 1 large egg, one large butter

Steps:

  1. Combine Dry: In a medium bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Combine Wet:In a separate small bowl, whisk the egg, then whisk in the milk and the melted butter/oil.
  3. Mix Together: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Mix gently with a spoon or whisk until just combined. Lumps are okay! Don't overmix.
  4. Cook: Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  5. Pour:Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the hot pan for each pancake.
  6. Flip: Cook for 2-3 minutes until bubbles appear on the surface and the edges look set. Flip and cook for 1-2 minutes on the other side until golden brown.
  7. Serve: Stack them high and top with your favorite syrup or fruit!
