Midnight Snack

9. Two-Ingredient Banana "Ice Cream"

ingredients: 2 frozen sliced bananas, 1 splash of milk (optional).

Steps:

  1. Place the frozen banana slices into a food processor or high-powered blender.
  2. Blend, scraping down the sides, until it becomes smooth and creamy like soft-serve ice cream. (Add a tiny splash of milk if needed to get it moving).
  3. Eat immediately!

10. Quick Avocado Toast

ingredients: 1 slice of toast, 1/4 ripe avocado, salt, pepper.

Steps:

  1. Toast a slice of bread.
  2. Mash the avocado on the toast with a fork.
  3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Add a dash of hot sauce or red pepper flakes for heat.
