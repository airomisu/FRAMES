5. Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi

ingredients: 1 lb raw shrimp (peeled and deveined), 2 cloves garlic, minced, 2 tablespoons butter (or olive oil) and 1 lemon (juiced) Serving: Cooked pasta or rice (optional)

Steps:

  1. Prep: Melt the butter (or heat the oil) in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 30 seconds until fragrant (do not let it burn).
  2. Cook:Add the raw shrimp to the pan. Cook for about 2-3 minutes per side, until the shrimp turn pink and opaque.
  3. Finish: Squeeze the fresh lemon juice over the shrimp. Stir everything together. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Finish: Serve over rice. Garnish with a sprinkle of fresh parsley if you have it!

6. Fried Chicken

ingredients: 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, 1 egg + 1 tablespoon milk, whisked , 1/2 cup flour + 1/2 tsp salt + 1/2 tsp pepper + 1/2 tsp garlic powder (or any seasoning you like) Oil: 1/4 cup vegetable or canola oil (for shallow frying)

Steps:

  1. Prep:Slice the chicken breasts into thinner cutlets. Pat them dry with a paper towel.
  2. Set:Place the whisked egg mixture in one shallow dish and the seasoned flour mix in a second shallow dish.
  3. Heat: Pour oil into a large skillet until it's about $1/4$ inch deep. Heat over medium-high heat until shimmering.
  4. Coat:Dip one chicken cutlet into the egg wash, let the excess drip off, then immediately press it into the flour mix to fully coat both sides.!
  5. Fry:Carefully place the coated chicken into the hot oil. Cook for about 3-4 minutes per side until the crust is golden brown and crispy and the chicken is cooked through (no pink inside).
  6. Serve:Remove the chicken and place it on a plate lined with a paper towel to drain excess oil. Serve immediately!
