7. Microwave Mug Brownie

ingredients: 4 Tbsp of flour, 4 Tbsp of sugar, 2 Tbsp Cocoa powder,3 tbsp of milk, 3 Tbsp of oil

Steps:

  1. Prep: In a large mug, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and salt.
  2. Cook:Pour in the milk and oil. Stir until just combined into a smooth batter.
  3. Microwave: : Cook in the microwave on high for 60 to 90 seconds. (Start with 60 seconds; if it looks too wet, add 10-15 seconds).
  4. Serve: Let cool slightly and eat straight from the mug!

6. No-Bake Peanut Butter Bars

ingredients: 1 cup of Graham Cracker Crumbs , 1 cup of Powdered Sugar , 1/2 cup of Peanut Butter, 1/2 cup of Melted Butter/p>

Steps:

  1. Mix:In a bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, powdered sugar, peanut butter, and melted butter. Mix until it forms a uniform dough.
  2. Press:Press the mixture firmly into a 8 \times 8 inch pan lined with parchment paper.
  3. Chill: Place in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to set.
  4. Serve:Slice into bars and enjoy! (You can optionally melt chocolate chips and spread them on top before chilling.)
